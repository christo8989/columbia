Punishment

Dogs socialize different from humans. In short, they will not listen if they don't recognize you as the leader.

Recognizing you as the leader helps keep her safe in case she gets loose or she needs to listen in a split second.

Training

During training, we punish our dog with "tss", "ch", and "Ay!".

If she's not listening to a command, use those sounds to tell her to listen. Get LOUD if you need to.

When she listens, reward her.

Dog Walks

Use the collar with the plastic spikes (it sounds harsher than it is). If she's not listening, sharply tug left or right until she listens.

When she listens, reward her.

Safety First

I want to reiterate, this ultimately will keep her safe over the long run.
